How VANDE.SANKI Works

A 4-step process powered by custom 4-branch neural architecture

1

Image Upload & Preprocessing

Your image is resized to 224×224 pixels and normalized. This happens entirely in your browser's memory. No data leaves your device during this phase.

2

Multi-Branch Analysis

The image passes through 4 parallel neural network branches simultaneously:

🔍 Branch 1: Features & Structure 🎨 Branch 2: Texture & Patterns 📐 Branch 3: Edge & Boundaries 📡 Branch 4: Frequency Analysis

Each branch specializes in detecting different AI artifacts that human eyes can't see.

3

Attention & Merging

Channel and spatial attention mechanisms weight important features. All 4 branches merge into a combined 512-dimensional feature vector for final classification.

4

Result with Confidence Score

The dense classifier outputs a probability score (0-100%). Scores above 50% indicate AI-generated content. The entire process takes just 0.3 seconds.
