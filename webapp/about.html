💡 Project Inception & Planning

Project started with a clear mission: combat AI-generated fake images used for blackmail and exploitation. Named VANDE (from 'Vande Mataram' - victory/salutation) + SANKI (Synthetic Analysis & Neural Knowledge Intelligence). Architecture planned from scratch - no transfer learning, no pre-trained models.

Inception Planning Research

🎯 Our Mission

VANDE0.0.SANKI was born from a critical need: protecting people from AI-generated fake images used for blackmail, harassment, and exploitation.

As AI image generation becomes more sophisticated (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Flux), the ability to distinguish real from fake becomes crucial for digital safety. Particularly, we aim to combat the rising trend of AI-generated fake images of women used for blackmail.

We are committed to keeping this tool free, open-source, and accessible to everyone - because digital safety shouldn't come with a price tag.
