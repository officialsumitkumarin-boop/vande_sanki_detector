SANKI Privacy & Security Policy

VANDE0.0.SANKI.2 Model - Privacy Framework

Last Updated: January 10, 2026 • Version 2.0

1. The SANKI Promise

VANDE0.0.SANKI (Synthetic Analysis & Neural Knowledge Intelligence) is built with privacy as its core principle. Unlike cloud-based AI detectors that upload your images to external servers, SANKI processes everything locally on your device or on your private server.

🔒 Core Principle: Your images are NEVER stored, NEVER shared, and NEVER used for training. The analysis happens in real-time and the data is discarded immediately after processing.

2. Data Processing Architecture

  • Local Processing: Images are processed entirely on your machine or private server
  • No Cloud Upload: Unlike Google, OpenAI, or other services, we don't upload your images
  • RAM-Only Analysis: Images exist only in temporary memory during the 0.3-second analysis
  • Zero Retention: All image data is purged from memory immediately after analysis
  • No Logging: We don't maintain logs of your uploaded content

3. What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ No Image Storage: Uploaded images are never saved to disk
  • ❌ No Face Recognition: We don't identify or track individuals
  • ❌ No Metadata Mining: EXIF data is not extracted or stored
  • ❌ No Fingerprinting: We don't create digital fingerprints of your content
  • ❌ No Third-Party Sharing: Zero data sharing with any external entity
  • ❌ No Training Usage: Your images are NEVER used to train or improve models

4. Technical Specifications

⚡ Processing Time: 0.3 seconds
🧠 Model Size: ~15MB
📊 Input Size: 224×224 pixels
🔢 Parameters: ~4.5 Million

5. Terms of Use

VANDE0.0.SANKI is designed for ethical use only. By using this tool, you agree to:

  • Use the detector for identifying AI-generated content, not for creating it
  • Not use the tool for harassment, blackmail, or any malicious purpose
  • Understand that no AI detector is 100% accurate - results should be verified
  • Not reverse-engineer the model for creating better deepfakes
  • Use this tool to PROTECT individuals from AI-based exploitation

6. Disclaimer & Limitations

The VANDE0.0.SANKI model provides probabilistic analysis and should not be considered as absolute proof. Key limitations include:

  • Accuracy may vary based on image quality and AI generation technique
  • New AI models may produce images that evade current detection methods
  • False positives and false negatives are possible (though minimized)
  • Legal decisions should not be based solely on this tool's output
  • The model is continuously updated to stay ahead of new AI techniques
⚖️ Legal Note: This tool is intended as an aid for identification, not as definitive evidence. Always verify through multiple sources before taking any action.

7. Our Mission Against AI Exploitation

VANDE0.0.SANKI was created with a specific mission: to combat the rising tide of AI-generated fake images used for blackmail, harassment, and exploitation, particularly targeting women and vulnerable populations.

We believe technology should PROTECT people, not harm them. This tool is our contribution to making the digital world safer.
